# ROM

The Noxis ROM (or, Read Only Memory) is a fast way to get information from Noxis services. They are more efficient to pull from then communications, and are easy ways to get pieces of info you plan to retrieve at a fast rate.

You can see the current ROM of a Noxis service by looking at its upper text with Maker Pen.
